What are the benefits?

One of the best parts of peer pairing is seeing from a new perspective. The programmer, if he/she is not careful can spend massive amount of time alone doing things in a way that may be very unefficient or ugly. What is great then about peer pairing is having the opportunity to see how somebody else does something and to learn from their style.

The Driver

The driver types up the code and is responsible for provinding most of the sytax.

The Navigator

The Navigator guides the team by looking up how to do things online, assists in filling the syntax and sharing the responsibility for the project
